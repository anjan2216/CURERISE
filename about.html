Our Story

CureRise was founded in 2023 when our team witnessed firsthand how financial constraints prevented families from accessing life-saving medical care. We saw patients being turned away from hospitals, families selling their homes to afford treatment, and children suffering because their parents couldn't pay for surgery.

We realized that while there was immense goodwill and desire to help among people, there was no transparent, trustworthy platform that could connect donors directly with verified medical needs. Traditional charity models often lacked transparency, with donors unsure if their contributions actually reached the intended recipients.

That's when we decided to build CureRise - a platform that would revolutionize healthcare funding by ensuring complete transparency, direct impact, and holistic support including nutrition through our Food Bank initiative.
